Privacy Policy

Velocity AI (operated by JamesTech, LLC) · Last updated June 14, 2026

This policy explains how the Velocity AI internal reporting application ("the App") handles data. The App is a private, single-tenant tool built and operated by JamesTech, LLC ("we," "us") for our own internal business reporting. It is not a public or multi-customer product.

What the App accesses

With your authorization, the App connects to QuickBooks Online through Intuit's official API to read financial reporting data for the company that authorizes it, such as profit-and-loss summaries, income, and expense totals. The App requests read access only. It does not create, modify, or delete data in QuickBooks Online.

How we use the data

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used solely to generate internal financial dashboards and summaries for the authorizing business owner. We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose this data to any third party, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where the data is stored

Retrieved figures are stored locally in a private database on hardware controlled by the business owner. Access tokens are stored in a protected local credentials file and are never transmitted to any party other than Intuit during authorized API calls.

Data retention and deletion

Data is retained only as long as needed for internal reporting and can be deleted at any time by the business owner. You can revoke the App's access at any time from your Intuit account under Connected Apps, which immediately stops all further data access.

Security

The App uses Intuit's OAuth 2.0 authorization and transmits data over encrypted HTTPS connections. Credentials are kept out of source control and restricted to the operating environment.
